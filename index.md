## About

I am **Yahui Liu**, a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Soochow University, advised by [Prof. Zhenghua Li](http://hlt.suda.edu.cn/~zhli/) and [Prof. Min Zhang](https://zhangminsuda.github.io/cn_homepage/). My research focuses on natural language processing, especially syntax and semantics, semantic role labeling, frame semantic parsing, corpus construction, and annotation data quality control.

My work aims to build reliable linguistic resources and robust NLP methods. I have worked on Chinese semantic role annotation guidelines, multi-domain predicate-argument corpus construction, annotation error detection, and data quality improvement for NLP. I have published first-author work in **ESWA** and **NAACL**, and my systems have won top-three awards in CCL Chinese Frame Semantic Parsing evaluations for three consecutive years.

## Research Interests

- Syntax and semantics
- Semantic role labeling and frame semantic parsing
- Corpus construction and annotation guideline design
- Annotation error detection and data quality control
- LLM-assisted data quality improvement for NLP
